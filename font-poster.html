Font Poster:
Frutiger

Brief:

design a poster that educates on and displays the key characteristics of the frutiger font for a class assignment

Solution:

  • in my research of the font, I found that it was designed to be legible from far distaces at a variety of sizes. I emphasized this quality by including a variety of sizes and angles to the displayed font.
  • in order to showcase the font's mixed humanistic and geometric design, I highlighted a few characteristic on letters that showed geometric and humanistic qualities
